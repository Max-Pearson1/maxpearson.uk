About Max

A short introduction to who you are, what you care about, and why you are involved in politics and civic life.

I am a student with a strong interest in politics, public service, local government and youth representation. My work focuses on helping young people understand the decisions that affect them, encouraging democratic participation, and supporting practical community action.


Through school leadership, civic projects and local engagement, I aim to make politics clearer, more accessible and more relevant to people who often feel excluded from it.
